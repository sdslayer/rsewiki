Devastating Toothpicks

CLEARANCE LEVEL: 4

DESCRIPTION

RSE-006 refers to the collection of sixteen innate birch wood toothpicks, classified RSE-006-1 through RSE-006-16. Each instance of RSE-006 is in remarkable condition despite frequent testing, where intense force is applied into the RSE-006 specimen.

RSE-006 instances have the anomalous ability to completely eradicate most forms of inorganic matter depending on the force applied between RSE-006 and the object the specimen is in contact with. Observations have shown that RSE-006 is capable of fully destroying five-meter thick concrete walls with enough applied force, and further testing may be required to understand RSE-006's full extent.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-006 is contained within a reinforced steel toothpick holder within its own containment chamber in Location 01. RSE-006 is to be removed from containment, counted, and carefully observed for five minutes by a Level 4 Clearance researcher biweekly.
