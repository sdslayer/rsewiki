RSE-030

DESCRIPTION

RSE-030 is a dark gray T-shirt bearing the design of two black serpents following each other in a circle, similar to that of the Ouroboros seen in the Hindu cosmology. RSE-030 is unable to be damaged in any form, including being resistant to staining.

When viewed by any person, the subject will feel a strong obligation to put on RSE-030 and may go to drastic measures to do so. When applied, RSE-030 will trigger cardiac arrest in the subject wearing it within 10 seconds of putting it on. After neutralizing the person wearing it, RSE-030 begins to decompose the corpse inside of it until there are no traces left.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-030 is kept in its own containment cell in Sector 02 of Location 02. The cell has a provided observation deck with black tinted windows to prevent the anomalous effects of RSE-030. Any personnel working with RSE-030 directly are to be blindfolded before entering the containment cell. RSE-030 is being temporarily held in a heavily guarded make-shift containment cell in Sector 01 of Location 02. This is due to the manifestation of RSE-018 in the previous containment cell of RSE-030 on March 12, 2018.
