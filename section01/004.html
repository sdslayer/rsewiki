DESCRIPTION

RSE-004 is best described as a blue colored hexapod with a large ovoid for a head. It has the ability to phase through any walls thinner than 3 feet of solid material. This had led to many incidents of homicide caused by RSE-004, as it has recorded history of kidnapping people, taking them as hostages for anywhere between 3 days and 2 months, before ultimately killing them.

RSE-004 was captured on July 22nd, ███ after a 15-hour long chase in Colorado through combined cooperation of the RSE Foundation as well as numerous Colorado city and county police personnel. It was apprehended about 5 miles from the county center of Arapahoe County, Colorado.

RSE-004, as stated before, can phase through thin walls. However, it can not phase through people or any other living organisms. RSE-004 also, while not having any visible mouth, is able to emit human-like speech, although it appears to only know how to speak Italian. Attempts at using internet translation services are unsuccessful on RSE-004.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-004 is kept in a containment chamber at Location 03 in a cubic room. The walls are 15 feet thick and are solid reinforced iron. There is a motion detecting camera mounted in the corner of the chamber to monitor any attempts at breaching containment.
