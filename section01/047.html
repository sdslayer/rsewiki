DESCRIPTION

RSE-047 is a public YouTube video uploaded to the site on September 22, 2012 by an unknown uploader. The upload is 10 seconds in length and features a pure black screen with the words “WELCOME TO THE CHANNEL!” written in a compressed white Arial font. The original video link has since been lost, however channel Lix’s Archive reuploaded a mirror version on May 19, 2015. The video is known to incite extreme fits of laughter into those who watch it, lengths of which can vary greatly depending on the subject.
