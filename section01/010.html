DESCRIPTION

RSE-010 is easily one of, if not THE most dangerous RSE. RSE-010 is a hand permanently clenched into a fist covered in an inextinguishable flame. The heat of its flame has been observed reaching temperatures as high as 2000 degrees fahrenheit and as low as 0 kelvin. Its exact origins are unknown, but it is believed to have come into existence in the year 1334. Every 3 years, it created one member of the A.O.C, with ██████ being made in 1337. Once the final member was created in 1346, it began its war against humanity.

RSE-010 is dangerously intelligent, capable of creating technology that humans can’t even comprehend. It can communicate telepathically and through sign language. It is believed that Mount Everest was created where RSE-010 emerged from the Earth, and that the Mariana Trench was created when it struck the ground.

RSE-010 and the A.O.C are believed to have been stopped by ███ ███████████.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-010 is kept cryogenically frozen in a reinforced steel room in Location 01.
