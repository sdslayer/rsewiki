DESCRIPTION

RSE-035 is a cardboard cutout of former US president Ronald Reagan. The cardboard cutout itself is in great detail, almost 100% accurate to that of Ronald Reagan. The cardboard cutout is sentient, and claims to be containing the soul of Ronald Reagan. RSE-035 can name almost every event to the most minute detail in Ronald Reagan's private life, and claims to have witnessed all of it happen. Due to this, RSE-035 is more than likely officially containing the soul of the former US president. Since RSE-035 is literally a cardboard cutout, it cannot do any actual harm. However, RSE-035 is capable of movement, via gliding slightly above the floor at a slow speed.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-035 is allowed to freely roam Location 02, as it poses no threat to anything or anyone.
