DESCRIPTION

RSE-001 in its standard form

RSE-001 is a talking hand with smaller hands at its fingertips. It is known to greatly enjoy giving handshakes and having conversations with people. RSE-001 is permanently lubricated with an unknown brand of lotion. It is believed that the hand itself somehow makes the lotion itself. When asked why it needs to lubricate itself, RSE-001 responded “to keep my handshakes smooth.” Due to RSE-001 being just a hand, it’s handshakes are less like handshakes and more like sporadic up and down movements. These traits are how it earned the name Lotion Motion.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-001 is currently contained in the living quarters sector of Location 02. The subject is freely allowed to roam its living quarters block, and other test subjects are required to converse with RSE-001 at least once daily.
