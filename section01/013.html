RSE-013

Mayonnaise Holder

DANGER LEVEL: 1
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 2

[← RSE-012] [RANDOM] [RSE-014→]

DESCRIPTION

RSE-013 is an octopus-human hybrid with above average intelligence. It is known to hoard jars of mayonnaise in its freezer. This freezer has a seemingly endless capacity, as the jars disappear once the door is closed. Despite this, RSE-013 has been observed taking jars of mayonnaise out of this freezer. Researchers have tried to test the limits of the freezer, but its anomalous properties seem to only work for mayonnaise. When non-mayonnaise items are placed in the freezer, they merely remain there until removed by either RSE-013 or a researcher.

Despite hoarding mayonnaise, RSE-013 appears to dislike the condiment. RSE-013 poses very little threat to humans, except for the occasional tickling.

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-013 is located in a small room at Location 1.
