RSE-036 during Incident 03

DESCRIPTION

RSE-036 is a 25-year-old Caucasian male with the given name 'Kyle Ritters'. RSE-036 has curly blonde hair, sky blue eyes, and white teeth of grotesque proportions. Upon viewing RSE-036, he seems completely normal, but the anomalous properties come into play when identifying his ability. RSE-036 is unable to be harmed or killed. As a result of this, he attempts to commit mass homicide via different methods corresponding to what items he has access to. [see INCIDENT LOGS for more information]

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

RSE-036 is to be sealed in a 10x10m titanium cube, with 10m thick walls surrounding it. There is a single camera monitoring RSE-036 located at the top of his chamber. RSE-036 is to not be fed or given water - he has almost breached containment with these items on several occasions.

INCIDENT LOGS

Incident 01 - RSE-036 walked into a Mormon church and began to kick each person inside one by one, before being abstained. No one was injured.

Incident 02 - RSE-036 purchased a red 2014 Dodge Charger and began to drive at 75 MPH through the car dealership. One individual was killed, with 3 others suffering minor injuries.

Incident 03 - RSE-036 stole a private jet and began to ascend at an alarming rate. He then flew over 350 miles and began to nosedive into a small town located near Omaha, Nebraska. 23 people were fatally injured during the incident, with 12 others suffering minor injuries.
